# Dogmatism

Dogmatism, in the philosophical sense, is the position of those who claim to hold certain truths without submitting their principles to critical examination. The word comes from the ancient skeptics, who called dogmatic every school asserting positive theses about reality; it thus stands opposed to [skepticism](../glossary/skepticism.md). [Kant](../philosophers/kant.md) said he was roused from his dogmatic slumber by reading [Hume](../philosophers/hume.md).
