# Conatus

*Concept associated with [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md)*

The conatus is, for [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md), the striving by which each thing endeavours to persevere in its being, and which constitutes its very essence (*[Ethics](../works/ethics-spinoza.md)*, III). In human beings, referred to mind and body alike, this striving is called desire: far from a lack to be corrected, it is the power of existing that ethics seeks to increase. From the Latin conari, to strive.
