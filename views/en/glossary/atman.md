# Ātman (the Self)

Ātman names the innermost Self, the conscious subject that abides beneath the shifting flow of thoughts and sensations. In Advaita Vedānta this Self is not an individual ego but proves identical with [Brahman](../glossary/brahman.md), the one absolute. This claim stands in sharp contrast to the Buddhist [non-self](../glossary/anatta.md), which instead denies any permanent substance behind experience.
