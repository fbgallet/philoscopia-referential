# The Middle Way

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

The Middle Way is the course [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md) charts between two extremes: the unbridled pursuit of sensual pleasures and ascetic self-mortification, both of which he had tried without finding liberation. It takes concrete form in the [Noble Eightfold Path](../glossary/eightfold-path.md). Later, the tradition broadened the formula to a refusal of metaphysical extremes, between eternalism and annihilationism.
