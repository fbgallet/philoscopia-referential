# Volksgeist (spirit of a people)

The Volksgeist, or spirit of a people, is the singular genius of a nation, expressed in its language, its songs, its customs and its history, and forming the living unity of a culture. The idea comes from [Herder](../philosophers/herder.md), who holds, against the Enlightenment's universalism, that each people carries its own measure; the word itself spreads mainly after him, notably in Hegel. It grounds the [idea that a human being is first shaped by their culture](../axes/human-nature.md), and would feed modern nationalism.
