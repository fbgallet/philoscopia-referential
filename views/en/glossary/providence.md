# Providence (pronoia)

Providence is the rational, benevolent ordering of the whole by the divine [logos](../glossary/logos.md): for the Stoics, nothing happens in vain, and every event has its place in the reasonable concatenation of the world. This trust in a well-ordered cosmos grounds a metaphysical optimism and invites us to welcome fate rather than resist it. It stands against the pure chance that the Epicureans, by contrast, placed at the heart of things.
