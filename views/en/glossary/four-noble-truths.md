# The Four Noble Truths

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

The Four Noble Truths form the heart of the teaching [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md) delivers in his very first sermon: there is [suffering](../glossary/dukkha.md), it has an origin, [craving](../glossary/tanha.md), that origin can cease, [nirvāṇa](../glossary/nirvana.md), and there is a path that leads there, the [Noble Eightfold Path](../glossary/eightfold-path.md). Their structure is that of a medical diagnosis: an ailment, its cause, its prognosis, its treatment. They are less a dogma to be believed than a framework to be tested in experience.
