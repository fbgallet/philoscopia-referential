# Holism

Holism, from the Greek holos, whole, holds that the whole is more than the sum of its parts: society has laws of its own and shapes individuals more than it results from them. Durkheim makes it a principle of method, explaining the social by the social, and [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md) a metaphysics in which the individual can only be understood within the whole that carries them. It stands against [individualism](../glossary/individualism.md); the same opposition recurs in biology or medicine, between a global approach and the analysis of components.
