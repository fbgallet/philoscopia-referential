# Epochē (suspension of judgment)

Epochē names the sceptic's move of withholding assent, refusing to come down for or against a claim. Faced with arguments of equal strength ([isostheneia](../glossary/isostheneia.md)), the mind stops affirming or denying, and this suspension leads to tranquillity ([ataraxia](../glossary/ataraxia.md)). Pyrrho made it a way of life, and Sextus Empiricus set out its method.
