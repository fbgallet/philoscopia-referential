# Tychism

*Concept associated with [Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md)*

[Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md)'s thesis (from the Greek tuchē, chance) that chance is an objective reality operating in nature, not merely a measure of our ignorance. The laws of nature are not rigid constraints given in advance: they are habits that have formed and grown firmer in the course of an evolution, out of a primordial spontaneity. Against strict mechanism, tychism restores to the world a share of indeterminacy and growth.
