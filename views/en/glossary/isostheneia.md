# Isostheneia (equipollence)

Isostheneia names the equal force of opposed arguments: to any thesis the sceptic manages to oppose an equally solid counter-thesis. Neither prevails, and this balance of reasons induces suspension of judgment ([epochē](../glossary/epoche.md)). It is the central spring of the sceptical method set out by Sextus Empiricus.
