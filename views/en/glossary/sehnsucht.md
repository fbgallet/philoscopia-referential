# Sehnsucht (longing for the infinite)

Sehnsucht is the Romantic desire par excellence: an ardent longing with no assignable object, reaching toward the distant, the absent, the infinite, which nothing finite can fill. From the German sehnen, to pine, and Sucht, ardent craving. Far from a lack to be cured, this dissatisfaction is lived as the sign of the soul's vocation to the absolute; it tinges the whole Romantic feeling of the world with a sweet melancholy.
