# Concrete universal

*Concept associated with [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)*

The concrete universal (das konkrete Allgemeine) is, for [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md), the universal that does not stand above its cases as an indifferent abstraction, but particularizes itself and lives in its individuals. Against the abstract universal, a mere common mark obtained by erasing differences ("animal in general"), the concept, the state or spirit are wholes that differentiate themselves from within and exist only in and through their particular moments.
