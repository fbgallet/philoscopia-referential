# Spirit (Geist)

*Concept associated with [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)*

Geist, "spirit", is [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)'s central concept: not an individual psychic substance, but the spiritual reality that produces itself by objectifying itself in nature, history, institutions and art, and that gradually comes to know itself. It unfolds from subjective spirit (the individual) to objective spirit (law, morality, the state) and then to absolute spirit (art, religion, philosophy). The Absolute itself is Spirit: "I that is We, and We that is I".
