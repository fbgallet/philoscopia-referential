# Ataraxia (tranquility of the soul)

*Concept associated with [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md)*

Ataraxia is the absence of mental disturbance, the state of serenity that for [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md) constitutes the goal of the happy life, once vain fears are stilled and natural desires satisfied. The term is not exclusive to the Epicureans: the Stoics and above all the Sceptics also make it their aim, by other routes. It connects to pleasure understood as a [hedonism](../glossary/hedonism.md) of quietude, not of overflowing enjoyment.
