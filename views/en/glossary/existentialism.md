# Existentialism

Existentialism holds that, for the human being, "existence precedes essence": no one has a nature given in advance, each defines themselves by their choices and becomes what they make of themselves. This groundless freedom is also a burden: we are "condemned to be free", responsible, in anguish and without excuse. [Sartre](../philosophers/sartre.md) and [Beauvoir](../philosophers/beauvoir.md) carry its atheist version, after Kierkegaard and in the wake of Heidegger. It stands against [essentialism](../glossary/essentialism.md).
