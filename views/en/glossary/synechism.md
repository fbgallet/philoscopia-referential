# Synechism

*Concept associated with [Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md)*

[Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md)'s metaphysical doctrine (from the Greek sunechēs, continuous) that erects continuity into a regulative principle of thought: where others see sharp breaks, between mind and matter, self and world, idea and thing, synechism looks for continuous transitions. Matter is but « effete » mind, and every explanation should favour the continuous over the discontinuous.
