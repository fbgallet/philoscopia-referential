# Dukkha (suffering, unsatisfactoriness)

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

Dukkha is the first of the [Four Noble Truths](../glossary/four-noble-truths.md): conditioned existence is pervaded by a basic unsatisfactoriness. The term goes beyond pain, naming also the disappointing, unstable character of everything that passes, down to pleasures that do not last. For [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md), lucidly acknowledging dukkha is not pessimism but the starting point of a possible liberation.
