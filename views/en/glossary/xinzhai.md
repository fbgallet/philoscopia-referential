# Xinzhai 心齋 (the fasting of the heart-mind)

*Concept associated with [Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md)*

The "fasting of the heart-mind" is a discipline of the inner self: not abstaining from food, but emptying the mind of its fixed judgments, preferences and calculations until it grows clear as a mirror. This emptiness is no torpor but a receptivity: one so lightened responds spontaneously to whatever comes, neither anticipating nor retaining it, attuning to the [Dao](../glossary/dao.md). In [Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md), this turn toward [interiority](../axes/interiority.md) is the condition of an apt presence rather than a withdrawal.
