# Deism

From the Latin deus, "god", deism is belief in a single God founded on reason alone, without appeal to revelation, miracles or a Church. This « clockmaker » God created the world and gave it its laws but does not intervene in its course and is not disclosed in any sacred book. The natural religion of the [Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md), deism is distinct both from the [theism](../axes/god-existence.md) of the revealed religions and from atheism: [Voltaire](../philosophers/voltaire.md) and [Rousseau](../philosophers/rousseau.md) were its most famous voices, against the atheist materialism of a [d'Holbach](../philosophers/holbach.md).
