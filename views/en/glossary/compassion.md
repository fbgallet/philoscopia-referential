# Compassion

Compassion (Mitleid) is the immediate participation in another's suffering, to the point of making it one's own motive for acting. [Schopenhauer](../philosophers/schopenhauer.md) makes it the sole foundation of morality: it is possible only because the separateness of individuals, the [principium individuationis](../glossary/principium-individuationis.md), is an illusion, and because in another I recognize myself as the same [Will](../glossary/vouloir-vivre.md). It is distinct from the natural [pity](../glossary/pitie.md) of [Rousseau](../philosophers/rousseau.md), a mere reluctance to see suffering, and comes close to Buddhist karuṇā through its metaphysical background.
