# Family resemblance

*Concept associated with [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md)*

Family resemblance (Familienähnlichkeit) designates, for [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md), the kinship of the cases a single general word covers without any one feature being common to them all. Just as the members of a family resemble one another through a network of overlapping traits, the eyes, the gait, the temperament, without a single one being shared by all, "games" have no common essence, only criss-crossing similarities. The concept dismisses the demand for a definition by genus and differentia.
