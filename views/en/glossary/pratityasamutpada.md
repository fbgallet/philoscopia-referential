# Pratītyasamutpāda (dependent origination)

*Concept associated with [Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

Dependent origination is the central teaching of the [Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md): every phenomenon arises in dependence on causes and conditions, never in isolation nor out of itself. No thing stands alone, all is woven into a web of dependencies. [Nāgārjuna](../philosophers/nagarjuna.md) draws a decisive equivalence from it: what arises conditionally is devoid of [inherent nature](../glossary/svabhava.md), so dependent origination and [emptiness](../glossary/sunyata.md) say the same thing.
