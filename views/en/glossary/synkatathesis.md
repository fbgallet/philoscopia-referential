# Assent (synkatathesis)

Assent is the act by which the mind approves an impression, takes it to be true, or instead withholds. For the Stoics, this is exactly what is up to us: impressions force themselves on us from outside, but consenting to them or not belongs to our [prohairesis](../glossary/prohairesis.md) alone. Wisdom therefore lies in granting assent only to [kataleptic impressions](../glossary/kataleptic-impression.md), and refusing it to the hasty judgments from which the passions arise.
