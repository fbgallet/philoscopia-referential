# Sittlichkeit (ethical life)

*Concept associated with [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)*

Sittlichkeit, "ethical life", designates for [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md) concrete morality, realized in the institutions and mores of a community (the Sitten), as opposed to Moralität, the subjective morality of individual conscience and abstract duty. It unfolds in three moments: the family, civil society and the state. It is in it, not in the solitary inner self, that freedom becomes actual. From the German Sitten, mores, customs.
