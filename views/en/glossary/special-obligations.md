# Special obligations

Special obligations are the duties we have toward certain people because of our ties to them, family, friends, fellow citizens, and that we do not have toward strangers. For partialism, these obligations come first: the bond itself is the reason, with no detour through an impartial calculation. The ethics of care and the graded concern of [Confucius](../philosophers/confucius.md) place them at the heart of moral life. The objection is that, made into the only rule, they slide toward favoritism and clannishness.
