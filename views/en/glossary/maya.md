# Māyā (illusion, appearance)

Māyā names the power by which the one [Brahman](../glossary/brahman.md) shows itself as a manifold, ever-changing world. It is not sheer falsehood but appearance: the real veils itself and unfolds into diverse forms, which ignorance mistakes for ultimate reality. To dispel māyā is to know that beneath all multiplicity only a single reality abides.
