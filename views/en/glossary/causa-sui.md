# Self-causation (causa sui)

To be the cause of oneself (causa sui) is to be the origin of one's own being. [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md) reserved this status for the divine substance alone. A consequence for freedom follows: to be the ultimate author of my choices, I would have had to make myself, which is impossible. This is why Galen Strawson concludes that ultimate responsibility is out of reach.
