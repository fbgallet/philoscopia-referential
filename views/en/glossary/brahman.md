# Brahman (the absolute)

Brahman is, in Vedānta, the one ultimate reality: pure being-consciousness, without form or limit, the ground of all that exists. In Advaita Vedānta (non-dualism) it is even the sole true reality, the manifold world being only an appearance arising from [māyā](../glossary/maya.md). The innermost [Self](../glossary/atman.md) of each being is identical with Brahman, and recognizing this identity is [liberation](../glossary/moksha.md).
