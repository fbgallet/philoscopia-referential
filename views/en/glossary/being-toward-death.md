# Being-toward-death

Being-toward-death names, in Heidegger, the relation through which human existence understands itself from its own end. Death here is not a mere event to come, but each person's ownmost possibility, which no one can take on in their place. Facing it, rather than fleeing it in everyday busyness, tears one from anonymity and opens the way to an [authentic](../glossary/authenticity.md) existence. It is one of the great [attitudes one can take toward death](../axes/death.md).
