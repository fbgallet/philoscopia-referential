# Existential risk

*Concept associated with [Nick Bostrom](../philosophers/bostrom.md)*

For [Nick Bostrom](../philosophers/bostrom.md), a threat liable either to annihilate humanity or to permanently and drastically curtail the unfolding of its potential. The category warrants priority attention to long-term dangers, artificial intelligence or biotechnology, central to cautious [transhumanism](../movements/transhumanism.md) and the longtermism it gave rise to.
