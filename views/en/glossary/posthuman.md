# Posthuman

A being descended from the human but whose physical, cognitive or affective capacities exceed ours so radically that it would no longer belong to the human species as we know it. For [transhumanism](../movements/transhumanism.md), the posthuman is the desirable horizon of self-transformation; the notion differs from the critical "posthumanism" of the humanities, which instead contests the centrality of man.
