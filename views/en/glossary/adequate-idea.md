# Adequate idea

*Concept associated with [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md)*

For [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md), an adequate idea is a true idea grasped through its causes, bearing the intrinsic marks of truth, as opposed to the mutilated and confused inadequate ideas we draw from ordinary experience. To know adequately is to understand things as necessary: this is the path of reason and of freedom, since a mind forming adequate ideas becomes the cause of its own affects instead of undergoing them.
