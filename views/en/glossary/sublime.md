# The sublime

The sublime is what, by its grandeur or power, exceeds all measure and our faculties: the raging ocean, the high mountain, the storm, the infinite. [Burke](../philosophers/burke.md) distinguishes it from the beautiful by the mixture of terror and pleasure it brings, and [Kant](../philosophers/kant.md) sees in it the awakening, before nature's excess, of the feeling of our supra-sensible vocation. Romanticism makes it the heart of its aesthetics, where tranquil beauty yields to the emotion of the boundless.
