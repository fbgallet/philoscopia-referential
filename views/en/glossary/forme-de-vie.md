# Form of life

*Concept associated with [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md)*

The form of life (Lebensform) is, for the late [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md), the ultimate bedrock where justification ends: the whole of the activities, reactions and practical agreements in which a language is rooted. "What has to be accepted, the given, is forms of life." The agreement that makes language possible is not an agreement in opinions but an agreement in form of life; this is the bedrock where "the spade is turned".
