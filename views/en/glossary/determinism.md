# Determinism

Determinism is the thesis that every event, including our choices, necessarily results from prior causes in accordance with the laws of nature. If it is true, our decisions seem fixed before we even deliberate, which threatens [free will](../glossary/free-will.md). For [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md), the feeling of freedom comes only from our ignorance of the causes that determine us; [compatibilism](../glossary/compatibilism.md), for its part, tries to reconcile the thesis with a form of freedom.
