# Amor fati

*Concept associated with [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)*

Amor fati, the love of fate, is the attitude of willing events exactly as they have happened, regretting nothing and wishing nothing otherwise. Present among Stoics such as [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md), the formula is claimed by [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md) in *[The Gay Science](../works/the-gay-science-nietzsche.md)* as the highest affirmation of life. It extends [the dichotomy of control](../glossary/dichotomy-of-control.md) by turning mere acceptance of the past into love.
