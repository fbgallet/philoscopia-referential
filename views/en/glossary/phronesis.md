# Practical wisdom (phronesis)

*Concept associated with [Aristotle](../philosophers/aristotle.md)*

Phronesis is, in [Aristotle](../philosophers/aristotle.md), practical wisdom: the disposition that lets one deliberate well about what is good for oneself and for life in general, and then act accordingly. Unlike theoretical knowledge, it follows no fixed rule but discerns the right course in the particular case, which makes it the virtue that guides all the others, notably in the pursuit of the [golden mean](../glossary/golden-mean.md). Faced with the rigid letter of the law, it knows how to aim at equity rather than mere legalism.
