# Hypostasis

A hypostasis from the Greek hupostasis, that which stands beneath, ground, subsistence is a level of reality that really subsists, a substance in the strong sense. In the Neoplatonism of [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), the term designates the three hierarchical principles that structure all reality: the [One](../glossary/the-one.md), [Intellect](../glossary/nous.md), and the [Soul](../glossary/world-soul.md), each proceeding from the previous one by [emanation](../glossary/emanation.md). Christian theology would borrow the word to think the three persons of the Trinity.
