# Intellectual love of God

*Concept associated with [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md)*

For [Spinoza](../philosophers/spinoza.md), the intellectual love of God (amor Dei intellectualis) is the summit of knowledge: through [adequate ideas](../glossary/adequate-idea.md) the mind grasps things under the aspect of eternity and so knows and loves God, that is, [Nature](../glossary/immanence.md). This [joy](../glossary/joy.md) accompanied by the idea of God constitutes blessedness, which is not the reward of virtue but virtue itself.
