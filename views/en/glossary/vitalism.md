# Vitalism

Vitalism holds that life cannot be reduced to the laws of physics and chemistry: living beings have a principle of their own, an impulse that inert matter is not enough to explain. Neither sheer mechanics nor a plan fixed in advance, life invents unpredictable forms. [Bergson](../philosophers/bergson.md) gives its most famous version with the élan vital, setting it apart from [mechanism](../glossary/mechanism.md) and [finalism](../glossary/finalism.md) alike.
