# Phantasia (impression)

In Stoicism, the phantasia is the impression or representation presented to the mind, an imprint left in the soul by things. Everything then turns on the [assent](../glossary/synkatathesis.md) that the [ruling faculty](../glossary/hegemonikon.md) grants or withholds: the [kataleptic impression](../glossary/kataleptic-impression.md) is the one that grasps its object and grounds knowledge. Not to be confused with the Humean "impression", a vivid perception opposed to ideas.
