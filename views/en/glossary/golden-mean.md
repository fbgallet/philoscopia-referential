# The golden mean

*Concept associated with [Aristotle](../philosophers/aristotle.md)*

The golden mean (Greek mesotes) is [Aristotle](../philosophers/aristotle.md)'s conception of virtue as a middle position between an excess and a deficiency: courage stands between recklessness and cowardice, generosity between prodigality and stinginess. This mean is not a fixed arithmetic average but the point fitted to the situation, settled by the [practical wisdom](../glossary/phronesis.md) of the prudent person. Virtue is thus a stable disposition of the soul aiming at the right degree in feelings and actions.
