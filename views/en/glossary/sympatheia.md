# Sympatheia

Sympatheia (cosmic sympathy) is the [Stoic](../movements/stoicism.md) doctrine that all the parts of the one living cosmos are bound by mutual affinity and interaction: nothing happens in it in isolation, every event being woven in advance into the fabric of the whole. Animated by a single [logos](../glossary/logos.md), the world is an organism whose parts answer one another; from this the Stoic draws consent to [providential fate](../glossary/providence.md) and duty toward the community of rational beings. [Marcus Aurelius](../philosophers/marcus-aurelius.md) makes it a leitmotif of the *[Meditations](../works/meditations-marcus-aurelius.md)*: all things are interwoven, and the bond is sacred.
