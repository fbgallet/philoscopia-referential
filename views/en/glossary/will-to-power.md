# Will to power

*Concept associated with [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)*

Will to power names, in [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md), the fundamental drive of all living things: not a mere will to survive nor a desire to dominate, but the tendency of every force to grow, to overcome itself, and to create. It accounts both for the values we posit and for life surpassing itself. The phrase Der Wille zur Macht, left fragmentary in his lifetime, was assembled after his death by his editors.
