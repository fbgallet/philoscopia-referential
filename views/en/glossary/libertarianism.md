# Libertarianism

Libertarianism, in political philosophy, starts from self-ownership: each person owns themselves and the fruits of their labor. A distribution is then just as long as it flows from free exchanges and legitimate acquisitions, so that coercive redistribution amounts to violating individual rights. [Nozick](../philosophers/nozick.md) gives its classic version in the wake of [Locke](../philosophers/locke.md), reducing the state to a minimum. Not to be confused with libertarianism about [free will](../axes/freedom.md).
