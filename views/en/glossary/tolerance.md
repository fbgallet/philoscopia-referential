# Tolerance

Tolerance is the refusal to coerce consciences: no one should be persecuted for their beliefs or opinions, first of all in religious matters. [Locke](../philosophers/locke.md) gives the argument in A Letter Concerning Toleration, faith cannot be commanded and salvation is no business of the state; [Voltaire](../philosophers/voltaire.md) makes it a battle against fanaticism. A cardinal value of the [Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md), it prepares freedom of conscience and the separation of the civil from the religious. Tolerance is not indifference: one may disapprove of an opinion and defend the right to express it.
