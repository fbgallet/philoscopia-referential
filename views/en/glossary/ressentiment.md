# Ressentiment

*Concept associated with [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)*

Ressentiment names, in [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md), the impotent rancour of those who, unable to act or take real revenge, turn their frustration into an imaginary one. Incapable of affirming their own strength, they devalue the strength of others and invent values that condemn what they cannot reach. This inversion of values lies, for him, at the root of slave morality.
