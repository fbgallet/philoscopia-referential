# Wu wei 無為 (effortless action)

Wu wei, literally "non-action," does not mean inaction but action without forcing: acting in accord with the spontaneous course of things rather than imposing a strained will upon it. It is the art of letting things unfold, like water that follows the contours of the land without struggling, by following the [Dao](../glossary/dao.md) and [ziran](../glossary/ziran.md). This wisdom invites us to tell apart what depends on our effort from what asks to be accompanied rather than mastered.
