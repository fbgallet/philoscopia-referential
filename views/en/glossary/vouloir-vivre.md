# Will-to-live

*Concept associated with [Schopenhauer](../philosophers/schopenhauer.md)*

The will-to-live (Wille zum Leben) is, in [Schopenhauer](../philosophers/schopenhauer.md), the very ground of the real: the Kantian [thing-in-itself](../glossary/thing-in-itself.md) identified with a blind Will, without reason or goal, manifest at every grade of nature, from gravitation to man, and of which our body is the immediate objectification. This willing is not a conscious choice but an endless striving, forever in lack, the source of universal suffering; to be delivered from it means to deny it, not to satisfy it.
