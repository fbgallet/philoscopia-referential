# Generosity (générosité)

*Concept associated with [Descartes](../philosophers/descartes.md)*

In *[The Passions of the Soul](../works/the-passions-of-the-soul-descartes.md)*, [Descartes](../philosophers/descartes.md) makes générosité the key to all the virtues: a rightful self-esteem grounded in the good use of free will, the one thing truly within our power. The generous soul, knowing itself master of its volitions, is led to esteem others as itself, to undertake things firmly, and to excuse others' faults. It is an ethic of mastering the passions through the will.
