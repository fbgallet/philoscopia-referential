# Effectual truth

*Concept associated with [Machiavelli](../philosophers/machiavelli.md)*

The “effectual truth of the thing” (verità effettuale della cosa) is, for [Machiavelli](../philosophers/machiavelli.md), the operative reality of a situation, what is actually done in it and produces effects, as opposed to its ideal or desirable image. To ground politics on it, rather than on imagined republics that have never existed, is the gesture of [political realism](../glossary/political-realism.md): one governs people as they are, not as they ought to be.
