# Logos

The logos is the rational principle that orders the cosmos and runs through it as an immanent reason. Heraclitus makes it the common law governing all becoming, and the Stoics identify it with the divine itself, a seminal reason of which every being holds a share. To live according to reason is then to align one's own [hēgemonikon](../glossary/hegemonikon.md) with this universal logos.
