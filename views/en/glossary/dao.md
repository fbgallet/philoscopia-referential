# Dao 道 (the Way)

The Dao, or "the Way," is in Taoist thought the ineffable source and spontaneous course of all things: a principle that precedes every distinction and that language cannot grasp without betraying it. It is neither a god nor a law imposed from outside, but the very movement of reality, to which the sage attunes through [wu wei](../glossary/wu-wei.md) by following [ziran](../glossary/ziran.md). It marks a ground poised at the edge of [transcendence](../glossary/transcendence.md) and immanence: beyond things, and yet wholly within their unfolding.
