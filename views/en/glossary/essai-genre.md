# The essay

*Concept associated with [Montaigne](../philosophers/montaigne.md)*

The essay is the literary genre [Montaigne](../philosophers/montaigne.md) invents with the *[Essays](../works/essays-montaigne.md)*: not a treatise expounding a doctrine, but the "essay", the trial of his own judgment on whatever subject. It is thought in the making, provisional and always open to revision, which seeks itself in the writing and allows itself to contradict itself from one chapter to the next. Taking one's own [inner experience](../axes/interiority.md) for matter becomes a method here, the author asserting no settled truth but testing, as he goes, what he can say of it.
