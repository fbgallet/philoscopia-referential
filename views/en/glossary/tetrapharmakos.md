# The tetrapharmakos (the fourfold cure)

*Concept associated with [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md)*

The tetrapharmakos is the Epicurean "fourfold cure", a condensed formula of the wisdom of [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md): the gods are not to be feared, death is nothing to us, pleasure is easy to attain, pain is easy to bear. From the Greek, the "four-component remedy". These four maxims dismiss the major anxieties and open the way to [ataraxia](../glossary/ataraxia.md); they extend Epicurean [hedonism](../glossary/hedonism.md) as a therapy of the soul.
