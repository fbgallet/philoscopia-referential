# Mokṣa (liberation)

Mokṣa is release from saṃsāra, the cycle of rebirths and suffering, the ultimate aim of life in Vedānta. It is attained not through ritual or action alone but through knowledge: recognizing that the [Self](../glossary/atman.md) is identical with [Brahman](../glossary/brahman.md). It is akin to, though not identical with, the Buddhist [nirvāṇa](../glossary/nirvana.md), another figure of deliverance.
