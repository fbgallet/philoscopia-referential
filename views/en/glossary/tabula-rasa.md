# Tabula rasa

*Concept associated with [John Locke](../philosophers/locke.md)*

Tabula rasa, Latin for "scraped tablet", names in [Locke](../philosophers/locke.md) the mind at birth as a blank slate, with no innate ideas: all our knowledge comes from experience, through sensation and reflection. This claim grounds [empiricism](../axes/knowledge-source.md) and stands squarely opposed to [innate ideas](../glossary/innate-ideas.md). It also implies that what we become owes much to education and environment rather than to a nature given in advance.
