# Representation

Representation (Vorstellung) names the object as it presents itself to a subject, under the forms of space, time, and causality, as opposed to the thing as it might be in itself. The term is central in [Kant](../philosophers/kant.md), but [Schopenhauer](../philosophers/schopenhauer.md) makes it the first word of his system, 'the world is my representation': a phenomenon wholly relative to a knowing subject, whose reverse side, grasped through our own body, is the [will-to-live](../glossary/vouloir-vivre.md).
