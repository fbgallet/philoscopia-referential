# Aufhebung

*Concept associated with [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)*

Aufhebung ("sublation", sometimes "supersession") is, for [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md), the central gesture of the dialectic: the German verb aufheben has a double sense, both to negate and to preserve, which Hegel calls "one of the most important concepts in philosophy". A figure of thought, once contradicted, is not simply cancelled: it is annulled in its one-sidedness, kept in what was true in it, and raised to a higher level. From the German aufheben, to lift, to abolish and to preserve.
