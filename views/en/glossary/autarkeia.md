# Autarkeia (self-sufficiency)

Autarkeia names the sage's self-sufficiency, their independence from external goods: wealth, reputation, and comfort are not required for happiness. Well-being rests on what is up to us ([the dichotomy of control](../glossary/dichotomy-of-control.md)), not on circumstances. It is central to the Cynics, such as Diogenes, and to the Stoics, and goes hand in hand with an unshakeable calm ([apatheia](../glossary/apatheia.md)).
