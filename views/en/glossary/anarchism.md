# Anarchism

Anarchism holds that any authority imposed from above, the state first of all, is an illegitimate domination to be shaken off. It calls not for chaos but for another order, founded on free association, mutual aid and federalism: society can coordinate itself without masters. Proudhon and then Bakunin gave its major nineteenth-century versions, across mutualist, collectivist and individualist currents.
