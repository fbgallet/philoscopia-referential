# Skepticism

Skepticism is the philosophical attitude that suspends or withholds assent, on the grounds that our faculties guarantee [no definitive certainty](../axes/certainty.md). From Pyrrho to [Hume](../philosophers/hume.md), the skeptic examines reasons for doubting rather than affirming, and in this stands opposed to [dogmatism](../glossary/dogmatism.md). For the ancients, this suspension of judgment was no dead end: it aimed at tranquility of mind.
