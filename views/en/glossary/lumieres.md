# Enlightenment

The Enlightenment (in German **Aufklärung**) names both an eighteenth-century intellectual movement and the ideal that drives it: the public and autonomous use of reason against prejudice, authority and superstition. [Kant](../philosophers/kant.md) gives its most famous definition, « man's emergence from his self-incurred immaturity », and the motto « Sapere aude », dare to know. To think for oneself rather than be led: such is the gesture that links confidence in [science](../axes/science-place.md), [progress](../axes/conservatism-progressism.md) and emancipation.
