# Communitarianism

Communitarianism, in political philosophy, challenges the picture of the abstract, self-sufficient individual: our identity, values and attachments come to us from the concrete communities that shaped us, family, nation, culture. Loyalty to one's own is not a prejudice to overcome but a virtue, and the real source of solidarity. [Walzer](../philosophers/walzer.md), MacIntyre, Taylor and Sandel formulate it in the 1980s against the liberalism of [Rawls](../philosophers/rawls.md). It stands against [cosmopolitanism](../glossary/cosmopolitanism.md).
