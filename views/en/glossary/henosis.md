# Henōsis (mystical union)

*Concept associated with [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md)*

Henōsis from the Greek henôsis, unification, from hen, one designates, in [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), the soul's union with the [One](../glossary/the-one.md) at the term of its ascent. Beyond the contemplation of [Intellect](../glossary/nous.md), the soul, stripped of everything, even of thought, touches its principle in an instant where the duality of subject and object is effaced: "the flight of the alone to the Alone". It is the summit of the spiritual life, unsayable, which Plotinus claims to have reached himself a few times.
