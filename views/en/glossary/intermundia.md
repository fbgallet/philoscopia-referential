# Intermundia

The intermundia are the calm spaces between the worlds where, according to Epicureanism, the gods dwell. Blissful and perfectly untroubled, they take no part there in the course of things: they therefore neither created the world nor govern it, and there is nothing in them to fear or to hope for. [Lucretius](../philosophers/lucretius.md) takes up this doctrine of [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md) to ground a theology without [providence](../glossary/providence.md).
