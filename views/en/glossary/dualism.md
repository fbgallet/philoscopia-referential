# Dualism

Dualism holds that reality comprises two irreducible orders: matter, extended and measurable, and mind, immaterial and conscious. [Descartes](../philosophers/descartes.md) fixes its classical form by distinguishing the thinking thing from the extended thing, at the cost of a lasting problem: how can two such different substances interact within one being? It stands against the monisms of [materialism](../glossary/materialism.md) and [idealism](../glossary/idealism.md).
