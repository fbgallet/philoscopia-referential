# Fallibilism

Fallibilism holds a middle position on [certainty](../axes/certainty.md): knowledge is possible, yet none of our claims is ever definitively safe from revision. One can therefore believe on good grounds without ever claiming a closed truth. This path avoids both the pretension of [dogmatism](../glossary/dogmatism.md) and the paralysis of radical [skepticism](../glossary/skepticism.md), making revisability the mark of all honest knowledge.
