# Praxis

*Concept associated with [Marx](../philosophers/marx.md)*

In [Marx](../philosophers/marx.md), praxis is the activity through which human beings transform the world and, in the same movement, transform themselves. Against a philosophy that would merely interpret reality, it makes labor and collective action the place where humanity changes its own conditions of existence rather than enduring them.
