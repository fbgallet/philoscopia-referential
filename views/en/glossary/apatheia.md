# Apatheia (freedom from the passions)

*Concept associated with [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md)*

Apatheia is the state of the Stoic sage freed from the passions, those excessive emotions born of mistaken judgments about what is good or bad. [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md) does not preach insensibility: the point is to no longer be disturbed by what is not up to us, by correcting one's judgments. It is the emotional side of the [dichotomy of control](../glossary/dichotomy-of-control.md), a fruit of reason rather than a heart of stone.
