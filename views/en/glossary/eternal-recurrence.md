# Eternal recurrence

*Concept associated with [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)*

Eternal recurrence is the idea, advanced by [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md), that your life might return identically, in its every detail, an infinite number of times. More than a cosmology, it is an existential test: would you live this very life again, changing nothing? To say yes to it with joy is to reach [amor fati](../glossary/amor-fati.md), the love of one's fate.
