# Filial piety

*Concept associated with [Confucius](../philosophers/confucius.md)*

For [Confucius](../philosophers/confucius.md), filial piety (孝, xiào) is the respect and devotion owed to parents and ancestors, the first of duties and the root of all virtues. It is not reducible to obedience: it is the acknowledgment of a debt to those who came before us and gave us life, extended in the care of elders, the memory of the departed, and the faithful transmission of what one has received. Beyond the family, it traces a way of inhabiting time: to know oneself a link between the generations, heir as much as transmitter.
