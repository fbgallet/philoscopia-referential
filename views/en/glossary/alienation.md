# Alienation

Alienation names the fact of becoming a stranger to oneself: an activity or a power proper to human beings turns against them and dominates them like an external force. [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md) coins the concept, [Marx](../philosophers/marx.md) applies it to labor, where the worker no longer recognizes themselves in what they produce, dispossessed of the fruit and the meaning of their activity. [Simone Weil](../philosophers/simone-weil.md) gives a searing description of it in the factory. The word reaches beyond labor: one speaks of religious, commercial, technological alienation.
