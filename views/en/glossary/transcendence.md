# Transcendence

Transcendence designates what radically exceeds a given domain: a God beyond the world, values beyond facts, the other person beyond my representations. In metaphysics it qualifies a principle exterior and superior to the reality it grounds, and [the question of God's existence](../axes/god-existence.md) is its most classical stake. It stands point for point opposed to [immanence](../glossary/immanence.md).
