# Ziran 自然 (self-so, naturalness)

Ziran, "that which is so of itself," names the spontaneous, uncontrived character of things when they follow their own nature without constraint or artifice. It is the ideal of a life and a world that unfold of themselves, mirroring the [Dao](../glossary/dao.md) that the sage rejoins through [wu wei](../glossary/wu-wei.md). Far from setting the human against nature, ziran invites us to recover within ourselves the spontaneity that convention and calculation overlay.
