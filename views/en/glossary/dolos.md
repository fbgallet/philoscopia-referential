# Dolos (guile)

Dolos, in Greek, names the concrete ruse: the trap, the skilled deception, the stratagem that catches the adversary off guard. It is the work of [mētis](../glossary/metis.md), its tangible product: the wooden horse, a masterpiece of dolos, takes Troy where ten years of assault had failed. [Odysseus](../characters/odysseus.md) is its master, and the goddess Athena loves him for it.
