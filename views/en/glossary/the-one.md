# the One

*Concept associated with [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md)*

For [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), the One is the first principle of all reality, absolutely simple and ineffable, "beyond being" and beyond [Intellect](../glossary/nous.md): nothing can properly be affirmed of it, and it is identical with the Good. Everything proceeds from it by [emanation](../glossary/emanation.md), like a source that overflows without dividing or diminishing itself. Toward it the soul makes its return, up to the [mystical union](../glossary/henosis.md) that surpasses thought and duality.
