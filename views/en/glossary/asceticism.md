# Asceticism

Asceticism, from the Greek askêsis, exercise, is the discipline that voluntarily restricts the pleasures and needs of the body, fasting, chastity, self-denial, for the sake of a good held to be higher: purity of soul, self-mastery, salvation, lucidity. Found in Christian monasticism, Stoicism or the Indian traditions, it treats the body as an obstacle or an instrument to be tamed. [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md) will see in it an "ascetic ideal" that turns life against itself. It stands against the celebration of pleasures.
