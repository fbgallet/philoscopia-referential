# Egalitarianism

Egalitarianism holds that a society is just only if it corrects the inequalities for which people are not responsible, those of birth, talent or circumstance. Far from abolishing freedom, it sees in it the condition of fair cooperation among equals. [Rawls](../philosophers/rawls.md) refounds it on fairness, [Marx](../philosophers/marx.md) aims at "to each according to their needs", others at equality of opportunity or of capabilities. It stands against [libertarianism](../glossary/libertarianism.md).
