# Nirvāṇa / Nibbāna (extinction, liberation)

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

Nirvāṇa literally means "extinction," like that of a flame blown out: the snuffing out of [craving](../glossary/tanha.md), hatred and delusion, and with them of [suffering](../glossary/dukkha.md). It is neither a void nor a paradise, but an unconditioned state of peace that ends the cycle of rebirth. For [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md), it is the ultimate goal of the path, attainable within this very life.
