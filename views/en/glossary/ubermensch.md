# The Übermensch

*Concept associated with [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)*

The Übermensch, or overman, is in [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md) the one who, after the death of God, no longer looks for ready-made values in heaven but creates new ones, out of the earth and his own power. He is neither a biological type nor a tyrant: he is a horizon, a way of becoming the lawgiver of one's own life. *[Thus Spoke Zarathustra](../works/thus-spoke-zarathustra-nietzsche.md)* proclaims him as the very meaning of the earth.
