# Sati (mindfulness)

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

Sati is the lucid, sustained attention brought to whatever presents itself: the body, feelings, mental states and phenomena, observed without grasping at them or fleeing them. A factor of the [Noble Eightfold Path](../glossary/eightfold-path.md), it is the ground on which concentration develops and attachment unravels. For [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md), it is through this attentive presence, not through dispersion, that one clearly sees the [impermanence](../glossary/anicca.md) of things.
