# Divertissement

*Concept associated with [Pascal](../philosophers/pascal.md)*

For [Pascal](../philosophers/pascal.md), divertissement (diversion) names everything that turns us away from thinking about our own condition: gaming, hunting, noise, restlessness. It is not mere leisure but flight, for human beings, unable to stay quietly in a room, stun themselves so as not to face their finitude and unease. The notion gives an old name to a very present experience: an attention endlessly solicited that keeps us at a distance from ourselves.
