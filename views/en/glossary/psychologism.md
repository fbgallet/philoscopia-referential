# Psychologism

Psychologism is the thesis that reduces logical or ideal laws to mere empirical laws of the mind: the principles of logic would be only regularities of how we happen to think, variable and contingent. [Husserl](../philosophers/husserl.md) refutes it in the Prolegomena to the *[Logical Investigations](../works/logical-investigations-husserl.md)*: to make the [validity of the true](../axes/truth-nature.md) depend on a psychic constitution leads to a self-refuting relativism, since that very thesis would claim to be universally true. Against it he sets the ideal, timeless laws and their irreducibility to the psychic facts that think them.
