# Theism

Theism affirms the existence of a God who creates or grounds the world, usually personal, who cares for his creation. The conviction may rest on revelation, on experience, or on rational arguments such as the [five ways](../glossary/five-ways.md) of [Thomas Aquinas](../philosophers/aquinas.md) or [Anselm](../philosophers/anselm.md)'s ontological argument. It differs from [deism](../glossary/deisme.md), which admits a God of reason without revelation or providence, and stands against [atheism](../glossary/atheism.md).
