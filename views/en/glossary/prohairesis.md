# Prohairesis (the faculty of choice)

*Concept associated with [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md)*

Prohairesis is the faculty of choice and assent, that by which we judge, will, and grant or withhold agreement to our impressions. For [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md) it is the one thing wholly within our power, and therefore the true seat of freedom and of the person. It is the "up to us" pole of the [dichotomy of control](../glossary/dichotomy-of-control.md): nothing external can compel it without our own consent.
