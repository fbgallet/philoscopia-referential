# Indeterminism

Indeterminism is the thesis that not all events are fully fixed by their causes: some, as in quantum physics, are only probable. It opposes [determinism](../glossary/determinism.md). Note, however: indeterminism is not freedom. Chance is not choice, and replacing necessity with the roll of a die does not suffice to make us the authors of our acts.
