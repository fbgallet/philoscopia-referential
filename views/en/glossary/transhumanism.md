# Transhumanism

Transhumanism holds that humanity can and should transform itself through technology, biotechnologies, prosthetics, artificial intelligence, to enhance its physical and mental capacities, and even to abolish aging and death. It extends the Enlightenment faith in progress by applying it to human nature itself, taken not as a sacred given but as a perfectible starting point. [Bostrom](../philosophers/bostrom.md) is one of its major theorists. It stands against bioconservatism, which seeks to preserve a human nature not to be overstepped.
