# Revolt

*Concept associated with [Albert Camus](../philosophers/camus.md)*

Revolt is, in [Camus](../philosophers/camus.md), the lucid "no" set against [the absurd](../glossary/the-absurd.md): refusing the world's silence without either resigning oneself or committing suicide. Far from isolating, this refusal reveals a value shared by all human beings, captured in the formula "I revolt, therefore we are." Turned toward the present rather than toward acceptance of fate, it stands opposed to [amor fati](../glossary/amor-fati.md) and grounds a solidarity in the common condition.
