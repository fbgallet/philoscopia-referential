# Emanation

*Concept associated with [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md)*

Emanation is, in [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), the way in which all reality proceeds from the [One](../glossary/the-one.md) by superabundance, without choice, division, or impoverishment of the source, as light radiates from the sun. It unfolds the hierarchy of the three [hypostases](../glossary/hypostasis.md): the One produces [Intellect](../glossary/nous.md), which produces the [Soul](../glossary/world-soul.md), which orders the sensible world. It is no [mechanical](../axes/causality.md) creation but a derivation oriented toward the good, calling forth in return the reverse movement of [epistrophē](../glossary/epistrophe.md).
