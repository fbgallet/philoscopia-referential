# Technological singularity

A hypothetical point at which an artificial intelligence able to improve itself would trigger an explosive, uncontrollable acceleration of progress, beyond all human anticipation. Coined by the mathematician Vernor Vinge and popularized by Ray Kurzweil, the idea is central to [transhumanism](../movements/transhumanism.md), which sees in it the threshold of a shift toward the [posthuman](../glossary/posthuman.md); its critics denounce an unfalsifiable technological eschatology.
