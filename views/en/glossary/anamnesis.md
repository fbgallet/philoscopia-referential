# Anamnesis

*Concept associated with [Plato](../philosophers/plato.md)*

Anamnesis is [Plato](../philosophers/plato.md)'s thesis that learning is not receiving knowledge from outside but recollecting it: before birth the soul contemplated [the Forms](../glossary/theory-of-forms.md), and teaching merely reawakens this forgotten knowledge. In *[the Meno](../works/meno-plato.md)*, [Plato](../philosophers/plato.md) shows it by leading a young slave to recover a geometric truth through questioning alone. True knowledge thus comes from [reason](../axes/knowledge-source.md) turning back upon itself, not from sense experience.
