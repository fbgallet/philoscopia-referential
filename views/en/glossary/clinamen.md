# The clinamen (the swerve of atoms)

*Concept associated with [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md)*

The clinamen is the slight swerve by which atoms, in their fall, spontaneously deviate from the vertical. [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md) introduces this swerve to break a strict causal chain and so to save human [free will](../glossary/free-will.md) against rigid [determinism](../glossary/determinism.md). It is Lucretius who gives its most famous account in the *[De rerum natura](../works/on-the-nature-of-things-lucretius.md)*.
