# Hedonism

Hedonism makes pleasure the sovereign good and the ultimate criterion of a successful life. It can be prudent and calculating, as with [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md), who retains only simple and necessary pleasures, and its modern version inspires [utilitarianism](../movements/utilitarianism.md). Nozick's [experience machine](../thought-experiments/experience-machine.md) tests its central claim, the idea that only felt pleasure matters; in this it differs from [eudaimonism](../glossary/eudaimonism.md), which aims at happiness as fulfillment.
