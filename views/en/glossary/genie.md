# Genius

Genius is the original creative power that does not receive its rules from without but invents them: through it, in Kant's phrase, nature gives the rule to art. Romanticism makes it its central figure, the artist-creator who copies no model and draws from himself a work without precedent. It thereby shifts value from [imitation to originality](../axes/art-nature.md) and founds the modern cult of the artist.
