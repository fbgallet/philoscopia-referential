# Materialism

Materialism is the doctrine that everything that exists is material: thought itself is not a separate substance but a product of the organization of matter. Sketched by the ancient atomism of Democritus and [Epicurus](../philosophers/epicurus.md), radicalized in the eighteenth century by [La Mettrie](../philosophers/la-mettrie.md) and d'Holbach, it lives on today as physicalism. It stands against [idealism](../glossary/idealism.md) and [dualism](../glossary/dualism.md) alike, and has nothing to do with the everyday sense of the word, a taste for material goods.
