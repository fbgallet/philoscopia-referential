# Emergentism

Emergentism holds that the whole is more than the sum of its parts: from the organization of simple elements arise new properties, life, consciousness, that no law of the components let one foresee. Water is wet though neither hydrogen nor oxygen is: the property "emerges" at the higher level. [Aristotle](../philosophers/aristotle.md) foreshadows it with form, biology and the sciences of complexity renew it. It stands against [reductionism](../glossary/reductionism.md).
