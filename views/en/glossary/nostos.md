# Nostos (the homecoming)

Nostos, in Greek epic, is the hero's return to his native land after war or wandering: not a mere journey but the restoration of his rank, his household and his identity. It drives the whole *[Odyssey](../works/odyssey-homer.md)*, whose hero [Odysseus](../characters/odysseus.md) is its paragon, the one who refuses oblivion and immortality to regain Ithaca. From nostos and algos, pain, comes, indeed, our word nostalgia, the ache of one deprived of it.
