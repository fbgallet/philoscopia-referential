# Idealism

Idealism is the doctrine that reality is fundamentally mental or spiritual in nature: the world does not exist independently of the mind that perceives or thinks it. [Berkeley](../philosophers/berkeley.md) gives its most radical version, [esse est percipi](../glossary/esse-est-percipi.md), [Kant](../philosophers/kant.md) a critical version in which the mind structures experience, and [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md) an absolute idealism in which the real is the unfolding of Spirit. In the philosophical sense, being an idealist is not pursuing lofty ideals: it is a thesis about what exists.
