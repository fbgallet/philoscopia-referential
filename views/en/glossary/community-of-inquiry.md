# Community of inquiry

*Concept associated with [Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md)*

In [Peirce](../philosophers/peirce.md), the indefinite community of all who inquire, whose final opinion, the one they are fated to agree upon at the ideal end of inquiry, defines [truth](../axes/truth-nature.md) and the real. No individual and no generation possesses the true on its own: it is the point of convergence of an inquiry pushed far enough, constrained by a real independent of our desires. This community is not the cause of truth but the place where it shows itself, which anchors [fallibilism](../glossary/fallibilism.md) in a collective, self-correcting project.
