# World-Soul

*Concept associated with [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md)*

The World-Soul is, in [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), the third [hypostasis](../glossary/hypostasis.md), which proceeds from [Intellect](../glossary/nous.md) as Intellect proceeds from the [One](../glossary/the-one.md). Turned upward it contemplates the intelligible; turned downward it orders, animates, and moves the sensible world, of which it is the principle of life and unity. Through it the cosmos is an ordered living being, the last faint reflection of the source at the lowest degree of [emanation](../glossary/emanation.md).
