# Hēgemonikon (the ruling faculty)

In Stoic psychology, the hēgemonikon is the ruling part of the soul, its command center. It is where impressions take shape, where [assent](../glossary/synkatathesis.md) is granted, and where judgment and [prohairesis](../glossary/prohairesis.md) are exercised. To watch over the hēgemonikon, keeping it upright and in line with reason, is the essential task of anyone who would progress toward wisdom.
