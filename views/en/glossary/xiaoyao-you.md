# Xiaoyao you 逍遙遊 (free and easy wandering)

*Concept associated with [Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md)*

"Free and easy wandering" is the ideal of a spirit that roams unhindered and aimless, loosened from usefulness, honors and fixed standpoints. The title-image of the first chapter of the *[Zhuangzi](../works/zhuangzi-zhuangzi.md)*, it names a freedom that is not the free will of a willing subject, but the playful ease of one who clings to nothing and espouses the course of things by following the [Dao](../glossary/dao.md). For [Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md), it is in this weightless roaming that the [sovereign good](../axes/sovereign-good.md) resides.
