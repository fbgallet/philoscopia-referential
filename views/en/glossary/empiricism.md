# Empiricism

Empiricism derives all knowledge from sense experience: the mind is first a [blank slate](../glossary/tabula-rasa.md) that the senses fill in, and even the most abstract ideas trace back to impressions. [Locke](../philosophers/locke.md) and then [Hume](../philosophers/hume.md) make it the weapon against [innate ideas](../glossary/innate-ideas.md); pushed to the limit, it leads Hume to skepticism about causation. It stands against [rationalism](../glossary/rationalism.md), for which reason has resources of its own.
