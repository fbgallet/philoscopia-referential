# Cunning of reason

*Concept associated with [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md)*

The cunning of reason (List der Vernunft) is the device by which, according to [Hegel](../philosophers/hegel.md), [reason realizes its end in history](../axes/history-meaning.md) without exposing itself directly: it lets individuals pursue their particular passions, the ambition of a Caesar or a Napoleon, and uses these private interests to accomplish, unbeknownst to them, its universal design, while they wear themselves out and vanish. Evil and passion thus become the instruments of freedom's progress.
