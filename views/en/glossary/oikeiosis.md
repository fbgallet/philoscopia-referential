# Appropriation (oikeiôsis)

*Concept associated with [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md)*

Oikeiôsis names the natural impulse by which every living being first recognizes itself as its own, then extends that concern to its kin and, circle by circle, to the whole of humanity. [Epictetus](../philosophers/epictetus.md) and the Stoics see in it the natural spring of sociability: self-concern, rightly understood, opens onto altruism and cosmopolitanism. From the Greek "oikeios", what is one's own, familiar, of the household.
