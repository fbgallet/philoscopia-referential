# Mechanism

Mechanism is the doctrine that everything in nature is explained by matter in motion and by laws, with no appeal to ends or vital forces. Born with the scientific revolution, it reduces living beings to complex machines: [Descartes](../philosophers/descartes.md) holds animals to be automata, and [La Mettrie](../philosophers/la-mettrie.md) extends the model to man. It stands against [finalism](../glossary/finalism.md), which explains nature by ends, and against [vitalism](../glossary/vitalism.md), which credits it with an impulse of its own.
