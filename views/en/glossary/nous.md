# Nous (Intellect)

Nous from the Greek noûs, mind, intelligence designates intellect or thought in Greek philosophy. In the Neoplatonism of [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md) it takes a technical sense: Intellect is the second [hypostasis](../glossary/hypostasis.md), the divine, eternal mind that proceeds from the [One](../glossary/the-one.md) and contemplates within itself the [Forms](../glossary/theory-of-forms.md), in a unity where thinking and being coincide. This hypostasis, a metaphysical principle, must be distinguished from "intellect" in the ordinary sense of an individual mental faculty.
