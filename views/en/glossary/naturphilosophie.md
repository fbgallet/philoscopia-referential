# Naturphilosophie (philosophy of nature)

*Concept associated with [Schelling](../philosophers/schelling.md)*

Naturphilosophie is the philosophy of nature developed by [Schelling](../philosophers/schelling.md): nature is not inert matter but a living organism, one and the same spirit that slumbers in the stone, dreams in the plant and awakens to itself in consciousness. Nature and spirit are but two degrees of a single creative power. Against the mechanism of the Enlightenment, it thinks the world as a [life that unfolds](../axes/causality.md) through degrees and polarities.
