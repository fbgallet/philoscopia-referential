# The Noble Eightfold Path

*Concept associated with [The Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md)*

The Noble Eightfold Path is the fourth of the [Four Noble Truths](../glossary/four-noble-truths.md): the concrete way toward the cessation of [suffering](../glossary/dukkha.md). It articulates eight factors, right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, [right mindfulness](../glossary/sati.md) and right concentration, that are cultivated together. It enacts the [Middle Way](../glossary/the-middle-way.md) that [the Buddha](../philosophers/buddha.md) recommends, on the ethical, mental and contemplative planes at once.
