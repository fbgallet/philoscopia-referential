# The sage (sophos)

For the Stoics, the sage is the ideal figure of perfect wisdom and virtue: his judgments are always right, and he is freed from the passions, having attained [apatheia](../glossary/apatheia.md). He welcomes everything that happens as in keeping with the reason of the world and locates his good only in what is up to him. He is a regulative ideal, almost out of reach, serving as the standard against which each person measures his own progress.
