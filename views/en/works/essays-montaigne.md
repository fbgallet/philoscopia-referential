# The Essays

*[Montaigne](../philosophers/montaigne.md) · 1580*

Montaigne's single work, begun around 1572 and reworked until his death, which invents the genre of the essay: a free examination of self and world, conducted without a system and always open to revision. In it Montaigne takes himself as his matter, "I am myself the matter of my book", to portray the human being in his changing condition. Among its hundreds of chapters are "Of the Education of Children", "Of Cannibals", "Of Cruelty" and the long "Apology for Raymond Sebond", the seat of his skepticism.
