# Zhuangzi

*[Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md) · 300 BCE · *莊子**

A composite collection of parables, dialogues and fragments of [Taoism](../movements/taoism.md), whose seven "Inner Chapters" are ascribed to [Zhuangzi](../philosophers/zhuangzi.md) himself. Through humor and paradox it unfolds the [spontaneous](../glossary/ziran.md), [effortless action](../glossary/wu-wei.md), the relativity of all viewpoints and free wandering (xiaoyao you): the butterfly dream, Cook Ding and the happiness of fish are its famous parables.
