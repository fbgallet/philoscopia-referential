# Enneads

*[Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md) · 270 · *Enneades**

The major work of [Plotinus](../philosophers/plotinus.md), gathered and edited after his death by his pupil Porphyry, who arranged the treatises into six groups of nine, whence the title. It sets out the metaphysics of the three principles, the One, the Intellect and the Soul, the procession of all things from the One and the soul's return to it. A founding text of Neoplatonism, it bridges [Plato](../philosophers/plato.md) and the Christian thought of [Augustine](../philosophers/augustine.md).
