# Discourses on the First Decade of Livy

*[Machiavelli](../philosophers/machiavelli.md) · 1531 · *Discorsi sopra la prima Deca di Tito Livio**

A vast commentary on the Roman historian Livy, published in 1531, this republican work is the counterpart to *[The Prince](../works/the-prince-machiavelli.md)*: [Machiavelli](../philosophers/machiavelli.md) draws from the history of Rome the conditions of liberty, the usefulness of conflict between the people and the great, mixed government, and the need to bring states back to their first principles.
