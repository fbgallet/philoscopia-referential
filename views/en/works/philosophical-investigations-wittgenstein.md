# Philosophical Investigations

*[Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md) · 1953 · *Philosophische Untersuchungen**

The major work of the second [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md), published after his death in 1953: a series of dialogical remarks that turn the Tractatus inside out. In it he criticizes the picture theory of language and hidden essences, and introduces language-games, family resemblance, meaning as use, the private-language argument and forms of life. One of the most influential books of the twentieth century.
