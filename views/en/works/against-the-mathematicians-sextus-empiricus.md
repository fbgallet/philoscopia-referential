# Against the Mathematicians

*[Sextus Empiricus](../philosophers/sextus-empiricus.md) · 200 · *Pros mathēmatikous (Adversus Mathematicos)**

A vast skeptical refutation of the claims of the learned and the [dogmatists](../glossary/dogmatism.md) by [Sextus Empiricus](../philosophers/sextus-empiricus.md): against grammarians, rhetoricians and geometers, then against logicians, physicists and ethicists. It contains the detailed attacks on the criterion of truth, the sign, the cause, the existence of gods and the good by nature.
