# Outlines of Pyrrhonism

*[Sextus Empiricus](../philosophers/sextus-empiricus.md) · 200 · *Pyrrhōneioi hypotypōseis**

A systematic exposition of Pyrrhonian skepticism by [Sextus Empiricus](../philosophers/sextus-empiricus.md), in three books. The first lays out the skeptical "way", its [modes](../glossary/epoche.md) and its goal, tranquillity; the next two turn against the [dogmatists](../glossary/dogmatism.md) and their claims in logic, physics and ethics. It is the chief source that transmitted the ancient skeptical arsenal to us.
