# Turing

*1912 – 1954 · British · [Analytic Philosophy](../movements/analytic-philosophy.md)*

British mathematician and logician, one of the founders of computer science: to get past the question "can a machine think?", which he judged too vague, he proposes a test, the imitation game, where what counts is not the machine's nature but its ability to pass for a mind.
