# Johann Gottlieb Fichte

*1762 – 1814 · German · [German Idealism](../movements/german-idealism.md)*

The first of the German idealists, he recasts Kant's criticism into a doctrine of the I that posits itself and posits the world as its obstacle: acting freedom, not the thing, is the principle of all knowledge.
