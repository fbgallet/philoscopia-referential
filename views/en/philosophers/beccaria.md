# Cesare Beccaria

*1738 – 1794 · Italian · [The Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md)*

Italian jurist and philosopher of the [Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md), author of On Crimes and Punishments, founder of modern penal thought against torture and the death penalty.
