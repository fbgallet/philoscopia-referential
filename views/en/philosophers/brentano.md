# Franz Brentano

*1838 – 1917 · Austrian*

Austrian philosopher and psychologist who revived the notion of intentionality as the mark of the mental; Husserl's teacher, he was one of the seeds of nascent phenomenology.

## Influence on

- **[Husserl](../philosophers/husserl.md)** (continues) : [Brentano](../philosophers/brentano.md), whose direct pupil [Husserl](../philosophers/husserl.md) was, transmits to him the thesis that all consciousness is consciousness OF something, [intentionality](../glossary/intentionality.md) as the mark of the mental, the seed of phenomenology.
