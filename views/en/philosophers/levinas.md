# Emmanuel Levinas

*1906 – 1995 · French · [Phenomenology](../movements/phenomenology.md)*

French philosopher of Lithuanian origin, a critical heir of [phenomenology](../movements/phenomenology.md), who made ethics first philosophy: the face of the other obligates me before all knowledge.
