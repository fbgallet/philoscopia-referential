# Habermas

*1929 – ? · German*

German philosopher and sociologist, a leading figure of the Frankfurt School and theorist of communicative action; in 'The Future of Human Nature' he opposes the liberal eugenics of biotechnology, which would compromise the autonomy of the programmed child and the moral symmetry between persons.
