# Frege

*1848 – 1925 · German · [Analytic Philosophy](../movements/analytic-philosophy.md)*

German logician and philosopher, founder of modern logic and one of the sources of analytic philosophy: he seeks to refound arithmetic on logic alone and distinguishes sense (Sinn) from reference (Bedeutung).

## Influence on

- **[Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md)** (continues) : [Wittgenstein](../philosophers/wittgenstein.md) came to philosophy through [Frege](../philosophers/frege.md)'s logic, whom he consulted before Frege sent him on to [Russell](../philosophers/russell.md). The Tractatus inherits his logical rigour, the distinction of sense and reference, and the context principle; the later Wittgenstein moves away from him, seeking meaning in use rather than in an ideal logical thought.
