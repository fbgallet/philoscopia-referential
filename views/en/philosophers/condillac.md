# Étienne Bonnot de Condillac

*1714 – 1780 · French · [The Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md)*

French philosopher of the [Enlightenment](../movements/enlightenment.md), theorist of sensationism: all the faculties of the mind derive from transformed sensation.
