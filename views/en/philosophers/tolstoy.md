# Leo Tolstoy

*1828 – 1910 · Russian*

Russian novelist turned religious and moral thinker: from the Gospel he drew a doctrine of radical nonviolence, non-resistance to evil by force, condemning war, the state, and the death penalty, and inspiring Gandhi.
