# Frank Ramsey

*1903 – 1930 · British · [Analytic Philosophy](../movements/analytic-philosophy.md)*

British mathematician and philosopher, dead at twenty-six. He founded the subjective interpretation of probability: a degree of belief is measured by the bets one is willing to accept, and the coherence of those bets obeys the laws of probability, the bedrock of Bayesianism.
