# Foucault

*1926 – 1984 · French*

French philosopher: he analyses knowledge as inseparable from power and from the discourses that, in each era, determine what may be held to be true.

## Influenced by

- **[Pierre Hadot](../philosophers/hadot.md)** (reinterprets) : [Foucault](../philosophers/foucault.md) borrows from [Hadot](../philosophers/hadot.md) the ancient spiritual exercises and the "care of the self", which he recasts into an aesthetics of existence, a self-fashioning Hadot will reproach him for having cut off from its universal aim.
- **[Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md)** (continues) : [Foucault](../philosophers/foucault.md) takes from [Nietzsche](../philosophers/nietzsche.md) the genealogical method: the critique of origins and of the universal pretensions of truth and the subject, which he unfolds into an analysis of the relations between knowledge and power.
