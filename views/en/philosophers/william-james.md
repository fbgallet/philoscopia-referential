# William James

*1842 – 1910 · American · [Pragmatism](../movements/pragmatism.md)*

American philosopher and psychologist, a leading figure of pragmatism: he measures the truth of an idea by its practical effects and defends the right to believe when the stakes are vital and the evidence undecided.
