# Russell

*1872 – 1970 · British*

British logician and philosopher: a rationalist and atheist, he holds religious belief to run counter to the demand for evidence and defends a morality without God.

- **[Leibniz](../philosophers/leibniz.md)** (reinterprets) : [Russell](../philosophers/russell.md)'s book on [Leibniz](../philosophers/leibniz.md) (1900) is a seedbed of analytic philosophy: he shows there that Leibniz's metaphysics rests on his logic, a programme he turns into a method of analysis.
