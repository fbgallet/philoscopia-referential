# Fukuyama

*1952 – ? · American*

American political scientist and philosopher, known for 'The End of History' and then for his critique of [transhumanism](../movements/transhumanism.md): in 'Our Posthuman Future' he calls it the world's most dangerous idea, threatening a human nature he holds to be the basis of equal dignity and rights.
