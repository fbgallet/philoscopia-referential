# Arne Næss

*1912 – 2009 · Norwegian · [Environmental Ethics](../movements/environmental-ethics.md)*

Norwegian philosopher, founder of "deep ecology": against a shallow ecology concerned only with human interests, he affirms the intrinsic value of all forms of life.
