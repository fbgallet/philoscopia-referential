# Xunzi

*310 BCE – 235 BCE · Chinese · [Confucianism](../movements/confucianism.md)*

[Confucian](../movements/confucianism.md) thinker, the counterpoint to [Mencius](../philosophers/mencius.md), for whom human nature is bad and the good is acquired through ritual and education.
