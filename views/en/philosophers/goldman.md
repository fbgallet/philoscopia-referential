# Alvin Goldman

*1938 – 2022 · American · [Analytic Philosophy](../movements/analytic-philosophy.md)*

American epistemologist, the leading proponent of reliabilism: a belief is justified if it issues from a reliable cognitive process (perception, memory, sound reasoning), even if the one who holds it could not argue for it. A pioneer of social epistemology.
