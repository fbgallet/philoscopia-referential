# Parmenides

*515 BCE – 450 BCE · Greek (Elea)*

Founder of ontology: his poem On Nature opposes the way of truth, Being one, immutable, ungenerated and imperishable, to the way of mortal opinion; to think change is already to grant being to non-being, which is impossible.
