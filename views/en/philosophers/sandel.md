# Sandel

*1953 – ? · American*

American political philosopher at Harvard, a communitarian critic of liberalism; in 'The Case Against Perfection' he opposes enhancement, which he argues betrays the 'giftedness' of life and the humility, solidarity and gratitude bound up with it.
