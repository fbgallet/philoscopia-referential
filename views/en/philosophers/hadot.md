# Pierre Hadot

*1922 – 2010 · French*

Historian of ancient philosophy who showed that ancient philosophy was less a system of doctrines than a way of life: a set of spiritual exercises, meditation, self-examination, attention to the present moment, meant to transform the one who practices them.
