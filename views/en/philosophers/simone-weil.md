# Simone Weil

*1909 – 1943 · French*

French philosopher and mystic, at the crossroads of labour activism, moral rigour, and a spirituality of attention. She makes pure attention, understood as an availability that suspends the self, the common root of knowledge, justice, and love of neighbour, and even a form of prayer.
