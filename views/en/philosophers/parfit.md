# Derek Parfit

*1942 – 2017 · British · [Analytic Philosophy](../movements/analytic-philosophy.md)*

British philosopher, a reductionist about personal identity: what matters is not the identity of a permanent self but psychological continuity, a thesis he drew from thought experiments such as teleportation and fission.
