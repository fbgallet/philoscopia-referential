# Crates

*365 BCE – 285 BCE · Greek · [Cynicism](../movements/cynicism.md)*

[Cynic](../movements/cynicism.md) philosopher, a pupil of [Diogenes](../philosophers/diogenes.md), who renounced his fortune for a life of voluntary poverty and taught [Zeno](../philosophers/zeno-of-citium.md).
