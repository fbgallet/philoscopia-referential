# Antisthenes

*445 BCE – 365 BCE · Greek · [Cynicism](../movements/cynicism.md)*

A pupil of [Socrates](../philosophers/socrates.md), often regarded as the forerunner of [Cynicism](../movements/cynicism.md), who set virtue and self-sufficiency above all pleasure.

## Influenced by

## Influence on

- **[Diogenes of Sinope](../philosophers/diogenes.md)** (continues) : [Diogenes](../philosophers/diogenes.md) takes up the ideal of self-sufficiency of [Antisthenes](../philosophers/antisthenes.md) and pushes it to the extreme, into a provocative practice of destitution and freedom.
