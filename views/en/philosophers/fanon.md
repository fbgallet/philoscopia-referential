# Frantz Fanon

*1925 – 1961 · French (Martinique)*

Psychiatrist and anticolonial thinker born in Martinique, engaged in the Algerian independence struggle: he analyzes colonization as a total violence that can only be broken by the violence of the colonized, the moment of their disalienation and of the reclaiming of their humanity.
