# Self-interest and others

**What place should we give our own interest next to that of others?**

*Others · value · bipolar*

Should I put myself first, leaving each to look after themselves, or place others' good before my own? Living only for oneself corrodes every bond; sacrificing oneself entirely dissolves the very self that loves. To take a position is to say what we owe ourselves, and what we owe others.

## Poles

### Ethical egoism

Each person ought to pursue their own interest and belong to themselves: self-sacrifice for others is not a virtue but a form of alienation. A successful life is built on self-affirmation, not renunciation.

*Stirner · Rand*

### Altruism

Living for others is the heart of morality: the good of others must come before our own interest. Encountering another person obligates and decenters us, and it is in this responsibility for the other that existence finds its meaning.

*[Levinas](../philosophers/levinas.md) · [Comte](../philosophers/comte.md)*
