# Against method

*Argument for [Place of science](../axes/science-place.md) : Epistemic pluralism*

**There is no single method that would guarantee truth: science itself advanced by breaking its own rules, and other paths reach a real it lets slip.**

*[Feyerabend](../philosophers/feyerabend.md), *[Against Method](../works/against-method-feyerabend.md)*, 1975*

## The argument

1. No set of rules has been followed by all good science and by it alone: the great advances, Copernicus, Galileo, were made precisely by breaking the canons of their day.
2. If even science does not owe its success to a fixed method, no method can be crowned as the sole gateway to truth: rationality is plural and historical, not a single algorithm.
3. Moreover, whole swaths of the real, the felt quality of an experience, the meaning of a life, what a tragedy or a rite discloses about the human, are grasped by art, narrative, tradition, and slip through the net of third-person measurement.

**⇒** So science is one powerful form of knowledge among others, not the measure of all: to disqualify art, lived experience, or traditional knowledge in advance is a dogmatism, not a result.
